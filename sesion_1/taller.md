## Practicando con print()
```python
print("basico")
print("1 + 1 =", 1 + 1)

print("5 - 2 =", 5 - 2)
print()
print("medio")
print("243 - 23 =", 243 - 23)
print("12 * 4 =", 12 * 4)

print("123.56 - 62.12 =", 123.56 - 62.12)
```
